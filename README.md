# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

![Dashboard Preview](Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

The Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends across multiple coffee shop locations. The dashboard enables users to explore business performance using dynamic slicers, Pivot Tables, and Pivot Charts for informed decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor customer footfall across store locations.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Track hourly and weekday order trends.
- Analyze category-wise and size-wise sales distribution.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Peak customer orders occur during the morning hours.
- Coffee contributes the highest share of total sales among all product categories.
- Large and Regular drink sizes account for the majority of orders.
- Hell's Kitchen recorded the highest sales among all store locations.
- Barista Espresso is the highest-selling product.
- Weekend customer orders remain consistently strong.
